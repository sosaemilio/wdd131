About Leon Cathedral

Leon Cathedral, also known as the Cathedral of Santa Maria de Regla, is a stunning example of Gothic architecture located in the city of Leon, Spain. Construction began in the 13th century and was completed in the 14th century, making it one of the earliest Gothic cathedrals in Spain.

Camino de Santiago

The Camino de Santiago, or the Way of St. James, is a famous pilgrimage route that leads to the shrine of the apostle Saint James the Great in the cathedral of Santiago de Compostela in Spain. The route has been taken by thousands of pilgrims for centuries and is known for its stunning landscapes, historic towns, and spiritual significance.
